Ranch↑

This section will give you a brief description, along with other info about the ranch salad dressing that is the second most popular in America just behind Caesar.

While typically being known as a salad dressing, Ranch is also very commonly used as a dipping sauce for veggies or an addition to things like wraps.

Balsamic is number 3 for most popular salad dressings in America. It is however much different than the first two, as it is much more of an oil based dressing than the others.

French↑

French dressing is a term originally used for any oil-and-vinegar-based salad dressing. Today however, it refers to a creamy ketchup-based dressing which varies in color from pale orange to bright red.
