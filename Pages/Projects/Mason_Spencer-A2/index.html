Fun Facts

  1. Creative Assembly, the creators of the total war franchise. Are the oldest game company in the UK


  2. Prior to its release to the public, one of their games named Total War Rome's engine was provided to the history channel and bbc2. They used it to create decisive battles for documentaries which depicted the most famous battles of the time.

What is Total War?

Total War is a game series where for a majority of their games they are putting the player into historical times in the human history such as Rome, the time of Attila, or the times of the Shogunate in Japan.
